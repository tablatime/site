What to Expect

  • A welcoming and supportive learning environment
  • Introduction to tabla fundamentals
  • Basic hand technique and posture
  • Simple rhythm exercises and guided practice
  • Opportunities for questions and feedback

What to Bring

  • Comfortable clothing
  • Water bottle
  • Enthusiasm and willingness to learn

Tabla placeholders will be provided during class when needed. Buying a tabla set is not mandatory unless you are looking for your child to continue fully. It is a big investment for a small workshop.
