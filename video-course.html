Video Course(underway)

Welcome to the online video course. These lessons are designed to help beginners practice outside of class and review the material covered during each session.

Lesson 1 – Introduction to Tabla

Learn about the two drums, proper sitting position, and basic hand placement.

Lesson 2 – Basic Strokes

Practice the first fundamental bols and begin developing consistent technique.

Lesson 3 – First Rhythm

Put together the techniques you've learned to play your first simple rhythm.
