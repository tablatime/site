About the Initiative

Mission

My name is Param Patel, and I am a high school student from San Mateo, currently attending Junípero Serra High School. I started this initiative after learning tabla for several years and wanting to share it with others in my community.

My goal is to introduce the basics of tabla to younger kids in a simple, structured way. The workshop is designed for beginners or kids who have never played before, with no prior experience needed, and focus on an introduction to rhythm and technique step by step. It would be amazing to be able to share the art with children and hopefully inspire them to continue learning the instrument in the future with their own conviction.

Main Points

  • Free beginner-friendly tabla workshop
  • No previous experience required
  • Step-by-step introduction to rhythm and technique
  • Supportive learning environment for young kids/li>
